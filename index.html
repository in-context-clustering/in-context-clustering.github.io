In-Context Clustering with Large Language Models


Visualization of and attention maps

We sample 30 data points of 2 clusters from t-distribution with $df=5, dim=2$. The numbers in the top right figure represents the order of the data points, and the color indicates the confidence of the prediction. The model is indifferent about the label of the first point due to permutation invariance, and then become more confident about subsequent points and points that are far from the other cluster. The attention maps in intermediate layers show clear patterns of different clusters.
